"Path of Light" Yoga School

Yoga lessons

Gentle hatha yoga

The lessons are intended for beginners and anyone who wants to gain a solid foundation for practicing yoga. During the 60-minute class, we learn postures and slow movements, focusing on three elements: asana (correct posture and positioning), pranayama (work with the breath), guided meditation that stimulates the mind-body relationship.

Yoga vinyasa

The lessons are intended for intermediate to advanced learners, but beginners are also welcome. In a 60-minute class that focuses on breathing-synchronized movements, you will inhale and exhale as you change yoga poses vigorously.

Regenerative yoga

During 90-minute classes, we learn very slow movements and long-term positions with leaning against a chair or a wall. These quiet, regenerative exercises are suitable for people of all skill levels. They can also be an excellent form of post-traumatic rehabilitation.

Relax in the savasana position

Prepare to enter the state of savasana. Lie down on the mat, place your arms alongside your body, with your palms facing upwards. Close your eyes, breathe slowly but deeply. Melt into the mat and let your worries float away. When you are done relaxing, roll slowly to your side and, with your arms, move to the cross-legged sitting position. Put your hands together as for prayer. Make yourself happy with what you have. Namaste.
